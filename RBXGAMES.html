How to Join

Everyone is welcome to join our Yeep’s Hide and Seek community team – no tryouts needed for regular members!

If you'd like to join our staff or management team, staff tryouts are required.

  1. Join our Discord server.
  2. Play Yeep’s Hide and Seek regularly.
  3. If applying for staff: complete the tryout/application process on Discord.
  4. Be active and respectful in the community.
